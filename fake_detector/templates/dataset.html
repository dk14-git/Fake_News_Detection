Dataset

In this Python project, we will work with a dataset called "news.csv." This dataset contains 7796 rows and 4 columns, providing ample data for training and evaluation. The columns include a unique identifier for each news article, the title, the text, and labels indicating whether the news is real or fake. With a size of 29.2MB, the dataset offers a significant volume of information to analyze. We will leverage this dataset to build an accurate fake news detection system, contributing to the fight against misinformation and promoting reliable sources of information.
